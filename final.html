Final

My proposal for the final project is loosely inspired by Fernando Pereira Gomes who is used to practicing his craft on the streets of New York, but recently, heâ€™s taken to shooting photos on the virtual streets of Los Santos, San Andreas, the fictional city of Grand Theft Auto V. When doing research on new media art I came across his work and was immediately interested in what he was doing. As a photographer, I saw how he used real-life photo techniques and implemented them in this virtual setting to capture the residents of Los Santos. I plan on taking a series of still life photography on cars from the video game Forza. This is because still, life photography is a type of photography that usually focuses on subjects that are inanimate. While it usually helps if they aren't objects that can move, the main requirement is that they be objects that don't move via their own volition. This allows me to capture a subject that is normally meant to be seen as something that is constantly moving and convert it into a still life to symbolize the beauty in it.
